About Me

I'm Daudi Mwanzia, a full-stack developer who focuses on using several computer languages to solve problems digitally. I support providing individuals with access to digital power through advanced, user-friendly computer technologies. I intend to use my knowledge to teach, design user-friendly systems, and aid others in the technology industry and other fields because I believe that doing so enhances my abilities and helps new problem solvers enter the realm of technology. Technical Expertise My skills lie in building web applications using Django, Flask, React.js, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy bridging the gap between the backend and frontend to create user-friendly and functional websites.

Beyond the screen

I have other interests in addition to coding because I think it's important to have fun. When I'm not coding, I may be found laughing out loud while watching comedies, engrossed in a racing or action game, or reading comic books. Two-wheelers are another area of great interest to me because they allow me to go across stunning scenery on my motorcycle or mountain bike. In my free time, I also write technical blogs for beginners using a beginner friendly approach.
